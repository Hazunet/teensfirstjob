Our Mission Statement

We are committed to empowering young people with the knowledge and skills needed to succeed. By providing accessible resources and support, we help youth get hired in their first job!


Who We are

We believe in the power of young people to create positive change. Our mission is to empower young people with the knowledge and skills needed to succeed. We are a non-profit organization dedicated to inspiring confidence and helping student find their first part time job! Our team is made up of passionate individuals who are committed to making a difference in the lives of young people. We help young people find job opportunities through our website. Thank you for your interest in Job Launch Pad. We invite you to learn more about our programs and get involved. "Donate today," "Volunteer," or "Contact us"
